MSL Monitor · Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

Public URL required by Facebook App Review and available to every user.

1. Who we are and scope

MSL Monitor is a product by MSL Estratégia that helps internal teams analyze Facebook and Instagram performance. This notice describes how we collect, use, and protect personal data and connected account data.

Data controller contact: privacidade@mslestrategia.com.br

2. Data we collect

  • Internal account credentials: name, corporate e-mail, and password (stored with PBKDF2 hash). Only authorized users can access the dashboard.
  • Meta data (after Facebook login consent): Page/Ad Account ID, Instagram Business ID, aggregated metrics (reach, engagement, followers) and public posts required for reporting.
  • Usage data: access records, timestamps, and request parameters for security and support.

We do not collect sensitive data or private content. Access tokens are used only to read authorized metrics.

3. How we use data

  • Authenticate users and provide secure dashboard access.
  • Generate performance indicators, charts, and reports for connected accounts.
  • Monitor stability, prevent abuse, and provide support.
  • Meet Facebook App Review requirements, including showing the authentication flow.

We do not sell, rent, or use data for third-party advertising.

4. Legal basis and B2B model

Processing is based on legitimate interest and contract performance to serve the controller's authorized team. Facebook Login is optional and allows reviewers or managers to verify consent to access metrics.

5. Sharing and third parties

  • Infrastructure: cloud hosting, database, and monitoring providers that support MSL Monitor.
  • Facebook/Instagram: only to read authorized data via the Graph API.

We do not share data with advertisers or commercial partners.

6. Retention and deletion

  • Account data and audit logs are kept while the organization uses the service or until a deletion request.
  • Historical metrics are stored for comparisons; they can be removed upon controller request.
  • Tokens and sessions expire periodically and can be revoked at any time by the user in Meta settings.

7. Security

  • All traffic is protected by HTTPS.
  • Passwords use strong hashing (PBKDF2) and tokens are stored securely.
  • Role-based access control (analyst/admin) and audit logs are in place.

8. Your rights

  • Access, correct, or request deletion of account data.
  • Revoke the app's access in Facebook/Instagram settings at any time.
  • Ask questions via privacidade@mslestrategia.com.br.

9. Changes

We may update this notice to reflect product or regulatory changes. The most recent version will always be available at this URL.
