MSL Monitor · Privacy Policy

How we handle data

Information for internal users and Facebook App Review.

1. Controller & contact

Controller: MSLE Estratégia. Contact: privacidade@mslestrategia.com.br.

2. Data we collect

  • Account: email, name, role, password hash (PBKDF2) or Facebook ID/name/email when social login is used.
  • Social metrics: data returned by Meta Graph API for connected pages, ad accounts, and profiles.
  • Technical logs: access/error/audit logs for security and troubleshooting.

3. Purpose

  • Provide internal dashboards for Facebook and Instagram performance.
  • Generate reports and analyses requested by the controller.
  • Fulfill security/audit requirements and Facebook App Review demands.

4. Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties, except essential providers (hosting, database) under contract and confidentiality.

5. Retention

Account data is retained while the user is active or as required by law. Meta metrics may be cached for performance/history within contractual limits.

6. Data subject rights

Request access, correction, or deletion via the contact email. For social logins, you may revoke access in your Facebook/Instagram account.

7. Security

  • Passwords hashed with PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256.
  • JWT tokens with configured expiration.
  • Role-based access controls and error auditing.

8. Cookies & local storage

We use localStorage only to store the authenticated session token. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

9. Changes

We will update this policy when needed. See the last updated date below.
